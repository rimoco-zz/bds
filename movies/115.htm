For their first live DVD, Canadian rockers Three Days Grace pulled out all the stops. Live At The Palace 2008 features hits from their 2003 self-titled debut and 2006's One-X, including "I Hate Everything About You," "Riot" and "Animal I Have Become" plus a cover of Alice in Chains' "Rooster." Bolstered by a literally explosive stage set, Live At The Palace 2008 will leave you wanting more.
